Polar Turtles is a game inspired by pool billiards that features...

  • a lot of fun for two players on one device
  • intuitive controls: just slide your turtle in the desired direction
  • cute turtles in 3 different colors
  • annoyingly happy background music

The game is best played with a friend on one device. If you can't find someone to challenge: A single player mode exists - but it is only half the fun.

The goal is to kick all your opponent's turtles into one of the holes by sliding one of your own turtles across the ice surface.
